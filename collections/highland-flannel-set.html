The Highland Flannel Set

Custom Order

The Highland Flannel Set is grounded in the kind of effortless, unhurried confidence that only comes from clothes that are simply well-made. The long-sleeve flannel shirt is built from a large-scale tartan plaid in burgundy, cream and warm grey — worn soft against the skin, with a western yoke at the chest, dual snap-fastened breast pockets, and mother-of-pearl snap buttons running the full placket.

It is paired with a pair of clean, slim-cut cream linen trousers — cuffed at the hem, fastened with a single button — that draw no attention to themselves but make everything above them look better. This is the set for a relaxed afternoon, a low-key event, or any occasion where looking good is effortless and intentional.

Fabrics Brushed flannel (shirt), Linen (trousers)
Colors Burgundy / Cream / Grey plaid (shirt), Natural Cream (trousers)
Silhouette Western long-sleeve shirt, slim cuffed trousers
Occasion Casual, Smart casual, Daywear
