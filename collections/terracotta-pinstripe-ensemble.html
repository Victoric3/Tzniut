The Terracotta Pinstripe Ensemble

Custom Order

The Terracotta Pinstripe Ensemble centres around a sleeveless double-lapel vest in a rich rust-terracotta tone running with fine ivory pinstripes. The vest fastens at the waist with a silver rectangular buckle belt, and features oversized pleated patch pockets at the hips — a design that reads both utilitarian and luxurious simultaneously.

The vest is designed to be worn over a crisp white dress shirt, with the matching cuffed pinstripe trousers completing the set below. The terracotta colourway is warm and distinctly warm-season, while the precision of the pinstripe and structured cut ensure this ensemble reads as sharp as any formal suit.

Fabrics Pinstripe suiting fabric (polyester-wool blend)
Colors Rust Terracotta, Ivory pinstripe
Silhouette Sleeveless belted vest, straight-leg cuffed trousers
Occasion Professional, Smart Casual, Events
